How do I cancel or reschedule my meeting?
On your profile dashboard, you can choose to either cancel or reschedule your upcoming FellowGenius session. You can request to cancel or reschedule any meeting at least 3 hours prior to the original scheduled time slot.
Do I get a refund, if I cancel a scheduled FellowGenius session?
In case you have to cancel a scheduled session, please do so at least 3 hours prior to the start of the session. Payment made for this session would be refunded within 5 working days. No refund would be offered if the session is cancelled less than 3 hours before scheduled start or in case of no show.
What happens if the Expert reschedules the meeting?
In case the expert requests to reschedule the meeting (at least 3 hours prior to the scheduled time), you can either accept the revised slot and connect with the same expert at the new time OR you can select another expert from our pool of curated experts and book a fresh meeting. In case you choose to set up a meeting with an alternative expert, you will need to pay (or be refunded) the differential amount as the case may be.
How to book a meeting with the same expert again?
Locate experts with whom you have connected in the past under the dashboard or sessions tab. Click on connect again to set up a new meeting.

Expert

What happens once I get registered as a FellowGenius expert?
Once you get confirmation from FellowGenius regarding successful registration as an Expert, complete your profile, as more details will allow the right set of learners to find you. Provide your available schedule and time slots. Accept the meeting requests from learners and conduct sessions. All payments will be credited within 14 working days of the completion of the sessions.
How do I cancel or reschedule my meeting?
FellowGenius recommends that experts keep their calendar updated as per the available time slots. In case of any change to an already booked session, the Expert can request the learner to reschedule the session at the next available slot, preferably on the same day. Requests to reschedule should be raised at least 3 hours prior to the scheduled meeting time. The decision to accept or reject the change is with the Learner, who can choose to reschedule or cancel the meeting.
How can I get more meeting requests?
Having a completed profile that mentions all domains and topics of expertise, will help the Learners to find you conveniently. Post session reviews by Learners will also form an important search tool for other Learners to find you.

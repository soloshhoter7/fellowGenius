About Fellow Genius

Learning On-demand!

Each one of us solves different problems in our work life every day. But sometimes we need support to find the right solutions, to brainstorm with others who have handled similar challenges before - someone who is an expert.

FellowGenius is an on-demand consulting platform for working professionals designed to meet their requirements for business expertise across functions in the conduct of their work.

With a vision to “uber”-ize the availability of industry expertise, we bring together industry experts from across domains and technical consultants to form a curated portfolio of services, across the entire gamut of enterprise specialization, offering their consulting services to professionals working in organisations, big or small, or even starting up.

Our Vision

Democratize the availability of industry expertise and technical know-how through an integrated platform catering to the enterprise value chain end-to-end.

Our Mission

Help professionals with best-in-class consulting support for their queries, enabling them to achieve workplace efficiency and optimization.

About Fellow Genius

On Demand Consulting

Each one of us solves different problems in our work life every day. But sometimes we need support to find the right solutions, to brainstorm with others who have handled similar challenges before - someone who is an expert.

FellowGenius is an on-demand consulting platform for working professionals designed to meet their requirements for business expertise across functions in the conduct of their work.

With a vision to “uber”-ize the availability of industry expertise, we bring together experts from across domains, so you can access cross functional expertise all in one place.
